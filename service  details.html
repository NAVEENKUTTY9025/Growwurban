Terrace garden setup

₹12,500 ₹18,900

Complete design and installation of a productive rooftop farm. Includes lightweight soil, raised beds, drip irrigation, and first set of plants.

  • Structural assessment & waterproofing consultation
  • Custom cedar raised beds (6'x4' or modular)
  • Lightweight soil mix + organic compost
  • Automated drip irrigation system
  • Selection of 15+ seasonal vegetables/herbs
  • 1-year maintenance support included

what's included

Everything you need for a thriving garden

site preparation

We inspect your terrace for load capacity, arrange for waterproofing if needed, and set up drainage layers before any soil touches the roof.

planting & design

Our team arranges plants for maximum sun exposure and beauty. We combine vegetables, flowers and herbs for a productive polyculture.

irrigation & care

All setups include water-efficient drip systems on timers. We also provide a season-by-season care calendar and organic fertilisers.
