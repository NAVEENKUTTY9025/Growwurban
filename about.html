From a rooftop dream to a urban farming movement

Groww Urban started on a tiny Brooklyn rooftop with just 20 square feet of growing space and a big idea: what if everyone could grow their own food, no matter where they lived?

Four years later, we've helped launch over 450 urban farms across 12 cities, proving that you don't need acres of land to grow fresh, healthy food. Our founder, Maya Chen, started with a single tomato plant that produced enough for her whole building – and the neighbors took notice.

🌍 planet first

Our commitment to sustainability

We don't just help you grow food – we ensure every part of our process respects the planet. From compostable packaging to water-saving systems, sustainability is at our core.

100% plastic-free packaging – all materials are compostable or reusable.
Water-efficient systems – our kits use 80% less water than traditional gardening.
1% for the planet – we donate 1% of sales to urban greening initiatives.
Solar-powered warehouse – our entire operation runs on renewable energy.
